ARTICLE 1 – PURPOSE OF THE CONTRACT
This contract pertains to the provision of assistance services for the Electronic Travel Authorization (eTA) application process for Canada, provided by the Service Provider to the Client, in exchange for a commission as defined in Article 7.
The services provided include, but are not limited to:
•	Verification of the Client's eligibility for an eTA (visa-exempt country nationals);
•	Assistance in completing the online eTA application form;
•	Verification of compliance with all information required by Immigration, Refugees and Citizenship Canada (IRCC);
•	Monitoring of the application until the eTA is issued (generally within 72 hours).
The Service Provider specifies that they are neither a lawyer nor a licensed Canadian immigration consultant (RCIC), and their role is purely administrative and supportive.

ARTICLE 2 – OBLIGATIONS OF THE SERVICE PROVIDER
The Service Provider undertakes to:
•	Provide the services with diligence and professionalism;
•	Continuously inform and advise the Client on the progress of the procedure;
•	Ensure service continuity throughout the procedure, even in the event of personnel changes.
It is specified that the Service Provider is an independent contractor with no subordinate relationship to the Client, responsible for their own administrative and legal organization.

ARTICLE 3 – PERFORMANCE TIMELINE
The Service Provider undertakes to assist the Client throughout the eTA application process, for a maximum duration of ${contract_duration}, renewable by written agreement of both parties.

ARTICLE 4 – MONITORING OF SERVICES
